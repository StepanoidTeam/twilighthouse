## LIGHTHOUSE

*A survival signal game made for Ludum Dare 59 — Theme: Signal*

You are a lighthouse keeper on a stormy night. Your only tool is the lighthouse beam — a rotating signal that cuts through the darkness.

Boats lost at sea depend on your signal to find safety. But the darkness hides more than just sailors…

---

## Controls

- `←` / `A` — rotate beam left
- `→` / `D` — rotate beam right
- `Enter` / `E` — confirm
- `Q` / `Esc` — back / pause

*On mobile: tap the on-screen ◀ ▶ buttons to rotate the beam*

---

## How To Play

- Rotate the beam to **light up approaching boats** — they use your signal to navigate safely to shore
- **Mermaids** swim toward the lighthouse in the dark — illuminate them to scare them off. Let 3 reach you and it's over
- **The Kraken** lurks in darkness and charges at you — shine the beam on it to drive it away
- **Police boats** are a different story — **don't light them up**. Every cop you illuminate confiscates a crate from the supply stash. Lose all 3 crates and the game ends very badly
- The **lamp burns out** over time — the beam narrows and flickers. Keep an eye on it
- Save **10 boats** to win

---

## The Story

Robert Pattinson is keeping a lighthouse and hiding a rather suspicious amount of cargo from Willem Dafoe. A quiet night shift turns into a chaotic battle against the sea, its creatures, and the authorities.
